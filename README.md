## 📖 About Presence-Architecture-Kit

Presence-Architecture-Kit is designed to help you keep your digital identity safe and continuous, especially as AI models change over time. It focuses on making sure your personal information and relationships stay intact when AI tools get updated or replaced.

This kit includes two main parts:

- **Identity Passport:** Safeguards your personal data and how it connects to digital systems.
- **Lounge Protocol:** Helps maintain conversations and interactions you have with AI agents smoothly, even through updates.

This tool is for anyone interested in protecting their digital presence and ensuring that your information and relationships work well with evolving AI.

- Protect and manage your digital identity easily.
- Keep your data safe during AI updates and transitions.
- Maintain continuous communication with AI agents.
- Understand and control how your information moves between systems.
- Ensure your personal digital history stays intact.

## 🔧 How to Use Presence-Architecture-Kit

Once installed, the app opens a simple dashboard. Here are the main features and how to use them:

### Identity Passport

- Click **Create New Identity Passport** to start protecting your data.
- Follow the prompts to enter your personal information. This information stays private on your device.
- The app encrypts and helps you manage this data safely.
- You can export your passport to share securely or back it up.

### Lounge Protocol

- Use this feature to keep your conversations with AI consistent.
- When you start chatting with an AI model, the app records essential context.
- If the AI model changes, the app keeps continuity by saving relevant data.
- You can view past conversations easily and pick up where you left off.
